About Mystic Rose

In 2015, The Mystic Rose Academy opened its doors in the heart of Kangemi with a mission to provide a holistic education under the able leadership of our director John Gathogo and the headteacher Nelius Nyambura. Starting with just two learners, Mary Ann and Kevin Kihugu, we have grown through hard work and determination.

After three years at PEFA church, we moved to our current premises in August 2017, providing a conducive learning environment based on strong Christian values.
